Questions

Good to know

What is Fauran?

Fauran is a bid-and-book app for home and repair work in Pakistan. You post a job, verified taskers nearby bid a price and arrival time, and you pick the one you want. "Ab Kam Hoga, Fauran" means the work gets done, right away.

How is this different from other home-services apps?

Most apps set a fixed price and you can't negotiate. On Fauran, you name the need and taskers compete by bidding on price and how fast they can arrive. That competition is the whole point.

Is it free for customers?

Yes. Posting a job and receiving bids costs you nothing. Taskers pay a small commission on jobs they win. It comes out of their own prepaid wallet, never added to your price.

How do I pay?

In cash, directly to the tasker, once the job is done. You share a 4-digit completion code to confirm the work, then hand over the agreed price. Fauran never holds your money.

Are the taskers verified?

Every tasker is manually reviewed against their CNIC and a live selfie before they're allowed to bid. Ratings and job history build on top of that over time.

When does it launch?

We're starting in Lahore and Islamabad, then expanding city by city. Join the waitlist and we'll text you the day bidding opens near you.
